## Overview

PDF Export generates a single PDF that includes every page in your documentation site. Open it from **Settings > Advanced > Exports** when you need a portable copy for sharing, review, or offline use. The export runs in the background, and you receive an email notification when the file is ready to download.

## Requirements

<Callout kind="alert">

PDF Export requires the **Standard** plan or higher. If your workspace is on the Starter plan, the Exports page shows an upgrade prompt with an **Upgrade to Standard** button instead of the export controls.

Your documentation must also be deployed before you can export it. If the site has not been published yet, the export button stays disabled until deployment is available.

</Callout>

## Generating an export

Start the export from the Exports page, then monitor progress while the system builds the PDF in the background.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Exports page" icon="settings" title-type="p">

  Navigate to **Settings > Advanced > Exports**.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the export" icon="download" title-type="p">

  Click **Export Full Site as PDF** to begin generating the file.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Track progress" icon="play-circle" title-type="p">

  Watch the progress bar as pages are processed. Progress appears as `X / Y pages (Z%)`, which helps you confirm the export is still moving forward.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for completion" icon="book-open" title-type="p">

  Keep the page open to see the export finish in real time, or wait for the email notification that tells you the PDF is ready.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">

A **Cancel** button is available while the export is running. Large documentation sites can take several minutes to finish.

</Callout>

## When the export is ready

Once generation completes, the Exports page updates with the download controls and export details:

- **Download PDF (X MB)** appears as the primary download button and includes the file size.
- **Generation details** show when the PDF was created and which user requested the export.
- **Export Again** lets you generate a fresh PDF whenever you need an updated copy.

## Export expiration

<Callout kind="info">

Previous exports expire after a period of time. When an export is no longer available, the page shows a message prompting you to generate a new one.

</Callout>
